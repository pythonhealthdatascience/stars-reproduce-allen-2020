Sharing research artefacts

This page evaluates the extent to which the original study meets the recommendations for the sharing of code and associated materials. We compare against two standards:

  • Best practice audit - requirements from the best practice audit by Monks and Harper (2023). The items in this audit were based on The Turing Way Community (2022), Taylor et al. (2017), and The Open Modeling Foundation (OMF) (2024).
  • STARS - recommendations from the recently published STARS framework by Monks, Harper, and Mustafee (2024).
